Login Page

This login page is designed for Freight Shield to accommodate multiple user types. Out of the four actors associated with our platform, three will utilize this portal for access, ensuring a seamless experience without the need for separate login pages. Note that the driver, being on the road, won't be logging in through this interface.


Signup Page (Shipper)

Our signup process is tailored to accommodate two distinct user profiles: Carriers and Shippers. Each profile has a multi-step registration process, meticulously designed to gather essential data for a seamless experience. Registration Process: Company Details: Provide your basic company information for identification and communication purposes. Business Details: Share specific details about your operations to help us cater to your unique business needs. Billing Details: Input your financial particulars for seamless transactions and invoicing. Terms and Conditions/Privacy Policy: Before completing the registration, please review and agree to our terms and the way we handle your data.


Dashboard (Shipper)

The Freight Shield dashboard, designed for Shippers, facilitates a seamless posting of load details. Once a carrier selects a load, the Shipper immediately receives a notification, complete with comprehensive details about the carrier company and real-time driver tracking. Beyond functioning as a marketplace, Freight Shield emphasizes safety, ensuring that participating companies adhere to stringent regulations and standards.
