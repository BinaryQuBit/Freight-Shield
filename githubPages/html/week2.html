Motivation

Ensuring public safety is the paramount objective of our project. As engineering students, we are deeply committed to upholding the highest standards of ethics and professional conduct in all our endeavors. We have identified a significant oversight in the existing system, which some companies exploit to their advantage. These entities compel truck drivers to operate beyond the legally mandated hours, posing potential risks to both the drivers and the general public. Addressing this flaw is not only a matter of regulatory compliance but also a testament to our unwavering dedication to safeguarding the well-being of all road users. We believe that by rectifying this issue, we can foster a safer and more responsible transportation ecosystem.
