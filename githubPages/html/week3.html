Getting Feedback from Customers

Progress Check in

Our team successfully engaged with representatives from Light Speed and 1005042 AB Ltd. Utilizing a questionnaire tailored specifically for drivers, we gathered essential data. From this information, we identified emerging themes and subsequently developed empathy maps for both clients to better understand their perspectives and needs.

Furthermore, we presented our project concept to the Department of Software System Engineering at the University of Regina. We are pleased to report that our idea received their full endorsement and approval.


Questionaire → Empathy Maps

A comprehensive questionnaire was developed, drawing insights from the current software utilized by drivers and companies. This not only encompassed existing features but also integrated potential enhancements introduced by our software solution. Both Light Speed's drivers and representatives from 1005042 AB Ltd. were interviewed using this questionnaire to gather feedback on their current software and anticipated changes. Based on the responses, we constructed Empathy Maps to further understand user perspectives. For a detailed overview, please refer to the provided resources.


Changes

After Careful consideration, we decided to change the name from "Load Lullaby" to "Freight Shield" as it fits with the theme of the application.
